    In Lublin Region, the horse chestnut is a treasure for pharmacy and cosmetology. Its seeds contain escin, which supports circulation and reduces swelling. In cosmetics, they have anti-aging and nurturing properties. Utilizing the horse chestnut is a local prescription for health and beauty.

    In processing, immature fruits, white flowers, seeds, and bark are utilized.

    Almost every part of this plant contains valuable substances.

    Main application: pharmacy and cosmetology.
