Obtaining herbal raw materials

We obtain most of our herbal raw materials from plantations located in Lublin Voivodeship, located in south-eastern Poland. This region is recognized considered one of the ecologically cleanest in the country. We also have a developed network of direct suppliers from other companies parts of Poland. Thanks to direct control and cooperation with farmers, using quality agreements and effective production methods we offer attractive prices and the highest possible quality.
